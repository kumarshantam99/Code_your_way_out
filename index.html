About Us

NSI Consultants is an organization that is committed to supporting businesses and NGOs through social investment.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives can be difficult to implement if businesses do not have the resources or strategy to apply properly. NSI effectively provides businesses with sustainable solutions for sustainable growth. Our consultants that are experienced in the NGO sector also provide assistance in order for organizations to expand their reach. Our end goal is simple: for our clients to Think NSI as we create sustainable solutions for greater social impact.

Modus Operandi

Based in Johannesburg, South Africa and actively operating in Norway and the United States. Our approach is two-fold as we’d like to change the culture of social investment; we identify and cooperate with businesses or NGOs working towards a social investment solution, but we also create sustainable solutions via our own project design. Our consultants are experienced in seeking investment and managing quantitative research in order to obtain sustainable solutions for businesses.

Experience

Our consultants all have experience in various backgrounds, whether that be in an NGO or the corporate sector, and all know the obstacles that they encounter when it comes to social investment. NSI seeks to create rewarding relationships through transparency and use innovative projects to do so.

Project Identification, Design & Management
Identification

NSI offers a comprehensive suite of project-based services. This begins with identification; matching the interests of investors and funding institutions with projects and initiatives. Services in this respect include:

  • Project preparation
  • Applications and identifying statutory incentives.

Design

Responding directly to the need for increased finance for projects in countries where NSI has presence; NSI’s project design services represent strategic inputs and capacity building, in the interest of enhancing socially valuable projects and programs.



Management

For corporate clients, project management services sees NSI acting as the reporting and monitoring instrument that maintains synergy between EPCM (Engineering Procurement Construction and Management), legal and financial functions.

Monitoring & Sustainability Reports

Sustainability reports, also referred to as ESG (Environmental, Social & Governance) reports, are key for businesses to be transparent with their stakeholders. These reports also enable businesses to procure new stakeholders. NSI collaborates with businesses to create KPIs and in assembling a year-end sustainability report. The measuring standards vary on each individual business model, as the GRI Standards and the SDGs developed by the UN are both suitable for measuring metrics.

Purpose Campaigns

Purpose Campaigns are used to create brand identity as well as raise awareness to different social and cultural issues. Businesses are capable of creating social impact and reinvent their business through purpose campaigns. NSI helps with Purpose Campaigns in the following way:


  • Utilize data collection before joining cultural conversations
  • Utilize media outlets and combine real world initiatives with marketing strategies
  • Take a Stand. CSR projects tailor made for your brand by NSI.
Site Visits & Trainings

When necessary, our team will visit with organizations in person or wherever a project is suited to take place. A site visit will be deemed necessary in order to evaluate the viability of a particular project. In addition, we also offer on-site or online seminars and training expositions on a variety of topics related to responsible business concepts so that your business is better equipped to face challenges.


Fundraising & Planning Assistance

Our consultants hold experience in non-profit management and fundraising, and we deliver results when organizations are in need of funding. NSI works hand-in-hand with clients and coordinates with sponsors to create innovative approaches to fundraising.


Grant Writing

Due to lack of time and commitment, non-profits and NGOs can find themselves strapped for funding and engaging with funding sources. Grants are an effective way to fund a particular cause. Business foundations, state and national organizations all provide grants. Our consultants provide a hands-on approach to creating a RFP. Our rate is flexible and dependent upon factors such as project deadlines and time contributed. We also work with the organization to ensure that their project comes to fruition with the grant. NSI contributes the following in the process before submission:


  • Grant Research
  • Writing
  • Editing
